Privacy Policy

Gaia Eyes Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gaia Eyes collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Gaia Eyes mobile app, the Gaia Eyes website, and related support or billing flows. Gaia Eyes is designed to help people notice patterns and context. It is not a diagnostic tool and it does not provide medical advice.

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact help@gaiaeyes.com.

1. Scope

This policy applies to the Gaia Eyes iOS app, the Gaia Eyes website, publicly accessible support pages, account and billing flows, and related services we operate.

If a third-party site or payment page has its own privacy notice, that notice may also apply when you are on that service.

2. Information we collect

Account and contact information

We may collect information such as your email address, account identifiers, plan status, and authentication/session information when you sign in, manage an account, or contact support.

Information you choose to provide

We collect the information you enter into Gaia Eyes, including symptoms, symptom updates, notes, reflections, check-ins, preferences, favorites, settings, support requests, and feedback.

Optional Health data

If you grant access, Gaia Eyes may read selected Apple Health data and related body-state signals your devices write into Apple Health. Depending on the permissions you choose and the devices you use, this can include categories such as sleep, activity, steps, heart rate, resting heart rate, HRV, respiratory rate, blood oxygen, temperature deviation, menstrual or cycle-related data, and similar health or body-state metrics.

Health access is optional. You can decline it, limit categories, or turn it off later in your device settings.

Location and local context information

If you allow location access or provide a ZIP code, we may use that information to provide local weather, air quality, allergen, and related environmental context. If you do not want to share live location, some features may still work with a ZIP code instead.

Technical, device, and usage information

We may collect technical information needed to operate and secure the service, such as device type, app version, operating system, request metadata, diagnostics, error logs, timestamps, and basic website or API request logs.

Billing information

If you purchase a subscription, billing and payment processing are handled by our payment providers. We may receive plan, subscription, billing status, and transaction metadata, but we do not store full payment card numbers on our own servers.

3. How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Gaia Eyes app, website, and support experience.
  • Generate personalized gauges, outlooks, current-signal summaries, support prompts, and related product features.
  • Sync account-linked information across sessions and devices.
  • Process sign-in, support, subscription, and account-management requests.
  • Send notifications, reminders, or follow-up prompts if you enable them.
  • Monitor performance, investigate bugs, prevent abuse, and protect the security of our systems.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

4. How we share information

We may share information with service providers that help us operate Gaia Eyes, subject to appropriate contractual and operational controls. Examples include:

  • Supabase for authentication, database, and storage services.
  • Stripe and related billing services for checkout, subscription management, and payment status.
  • Apple services and frameworks, including Apple Health and Apple Push Notification service, when you use those features.
  • Environmental data providers, such as weather, air-quality, and allergen sources, when local-context features require location or ZIP-based lookups.
  • Hosting, infrastructure, storage, and security providers that support the website, backend, and media delivery.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights or safety, to investigate misuse, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or asset transfer.

We do not sell your personal information as that term is commonly used, and we do not use Health data for third-party advertising.

5. Health data and sensitive information

Gaia Eyes uses optional Health and body-state information to add context to patterns and timing. We use that information to support app functionality, personalization, and user-requested features. We do not use optional Health data to provide medical diagnosis, treatment advice, or third-party advertising.

The app is designed around patterns, not certainties. If something feels urgent, severe, or medically important, use a licensed medical professional or emergency care as appropriate.

6. Cookies, local storage, and similar technologies

The website and related flows may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies for basic site operation, login state, security, support-page behavior, checkout, and user preferences. Third-party services involved in authentication, billing, or infrastructure may also use similar technologies as part of their own flows.

7. Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain account-linked features, operate support and billing flows, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations.

Some data may also remain temporarily in local device caches, background sync state, logs, or backups for operational reasons.

8. Your choices

  • You can choose whether to connect Apple Health and which Health categories to allow.
  • You can control location access, use a ZIP code where supported, or leave local-context permissions off.
  • You can edit or remove symptoms, notes, preferences, and notification settings inside the product where those controls are available.
  • You can contact us at help@gaiaeyes.com to ask about support, billing, privacy questions, or requests related to your information.

9. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information we process. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International processing

Gaia Eyes and its service providers may process information in the United States and other countries where they operate. Those locations may have data-protection laws that differ from the laws in your home jurisdiction.

11. Children’s privacy

Gaia Eyes is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to Gaia Eyes, contact help@gaiaeyes.com so we can review the situation.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of the policy. Material changes may also be surfaced through the app, website, or support channels when appropriate.

13. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or Gaia Eyes privacy practices, contact us at help@gaiaeyes.com.
